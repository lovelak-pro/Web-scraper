How to filter results:

In your browser's address bar, search for a website and add a filter in quotes.

Examples:
• example.com "@gmail.com" - Find Gmail addresses
• example.com "+92" - Find phone numbers starting with +92
• example.com "@company.com" - Find company emails

The extension will automatically filter and display matching results when you open the popup.
